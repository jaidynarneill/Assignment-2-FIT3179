Migrant Trends In Australia

Recent Migrant Arrivals

Over the 2016 to 2021 period, the average time spent in Australia by migrants is decreasing. Western Australia also has a higher proportion of individuals who arrived within the last decade compared to other states.

Better Income

Income across different states in Australia has shown significant changes for the better. Migrants who have been in Australia for longer periods tend to fall within higher income brackets, with South Australia leading in median income levels for long-term residents.

Data source: https://dbr.abs.gov.au/index.html
